Safety

Your Safety is Our Priority

Industry-leading safety features designed for outdoor adventure dating

Real-Time Location Sharing

Share your live location with trusted contacts during adventure dates. They'll see your exact GPS position on the trail.

Safety Check-In Timer

Set a timer before your date. If you don't check in, your emergency contacts are automatically alerted with your location.

Emergency SOS

One-tap SOS sends your GPS coordinates to emergency contacts and optionally to local emergency services.

Verified Profiles

Multi-step verification including photo matching, ID verification, and activity history from connected fitness apps.

Weather & Trail Alerts

Get real-time weather warnings, trail condition updates, and safety advisories before heading out.

Background Checks

Optional background check integration gives you peace of mind before meeting someone on the trail.

Experience Matching

Get warnings if a proposed adventure exceeds your skill level. Never get in over your head on a first date.

End-to-End Encryption

All messages are encrypted. Your conversations, photos, and location data are never accessible to third parties.
